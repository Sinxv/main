Once you reach level 99, you will unlock "Elrianode", and the 2nd guide of the "Dr.Pho's Guide", found in the middle top of your screen, your main objective on this stage, is to fully complete the first 2 Dr.Pho's Guides.

Elrianode Armor

On Dr. Pho's 2nd Guide, Lesson 1, you'll notice that the first mission is to Clear 3 Elrianode Region Dungeons, once you finish that, you will be able to claim the Restored Elrianode Armor and a 30 days Elrios Guardian Knights IB Accessories Set.
Both armor and IBs are really good for early game, since alongside your weapon, theyre a main source of Combat Power.

Be careful though, since the IB Accessories are temporal you'll need to save 550m~ ED to get 5500 EP Points, or alternatively, you could level up the same character multiple times, to get the accessories (Transferable via Luriel Bank), since every character gets more than 1000 EP from lv 1 to lv 99

Apocalypse-Type Weapon

Once you acquire the Restored Elrianode Armor you will notice that your weapon is not good anymore, since it's old by this point, so you will need to spam the "Add's Theory Dungeon" until you get the Apocalypse-Type Weapon Cube.

Notice that since the drop is RNG, this can drop the very first run or 10 runs, but is highly unlikely that it takes long. Also, you can upgrade the weapon to +10 Via Dr. Pho's 2nd Guide 16 Quests Cleared Prize *

Using this cube will give you a new weapon, which will be way stronger than the one you had previously.

Socketing

Your Armor and Weapon has something called "Sockets", which are empty when you get them. you can use Magic Stones for socket by pressing Inventory -> Magic Stone Enchant.
There are different tiers of normal magic stones:

T1: Magic Stone T2: Advanced Magic Stone T3: Refined Magic Stone T4: Sage's Magic Stone

It's highly recommended to keep your Sage's Magic Stones, as they are rare, instead, use your Refined Magic Stones to socket Elrianode Armor and Apocalypse-Type Weapon, as they are highly gifted throughout the Dr.Pho's Guide *

The stats you should socket are Maximize % and Critical %, if you get Critical or Maximize on 1 slot but all other slots are wrong, click the blue square to lock the slot and Click "Auto-Equip" to reroll all unlocked socket slots.
It's fine if you get low values on armor, instead search high values on weapon.

Wedges

Wedges are items that allow you to increase the max sockets of an armor or weapon by 1, "Ancient Guardian Wedge" is used for Armor, and "Ancient Iron Wedge" is used for Weapon. they can be crafted via Glave NPC with resources you get on Heroic Dungeon.
Heroic Dungeon is located in the Special Section of Dungeon List, {Include mention to Dr. Pho's lesson when i remember what lesson it was}, lets you recieve Wedges after doing Heroic Dungeon 2 times, so make sure to do them.

This is a system unlocked via quest, after you complete 7 dungeons from elrianode and onwards.
You can use the points you gain however you like, but the recommended way is to use them are:

For DPS

All Skill Damage Increase → Adaptation / Skill Cooldown Reduction → Polarize → Boss Damage Increase

For Support

Skill Cooldown Reduction → Skill MP Cost / Adaptation → All Skill Damage Increase → Max MP Increase

After reaching ERP 100, you get a quest that gives you a free El Resonance Page after completing 100 Elrianode or Higher Dungeons, it's highly recommended to use it for EXP Gain, as it can make leveling ERP up easier *

Accessory: Dimension of Sinister Intent (DoSI) Wings

On Dr. Pho's First Guide, Lesson idk youre asked to to do "Dimension of Sinister Intent", which is located on Dungeon List → Special, your task will be to Clear Dimension of Sinister Intent 6 times. You'll use this accessory for a LONG amount of time, and as you do Gate of Darkness you'll get a material that will allow you to upgrade the DoSI Wings.

Job Advancement: 3rd Class

Now that you have a new armor, new weapon and a couple of accessories, you should be able to easily advance to 3rd Class.
Tip: you can use the Party Finder to try to gather people to help you, don't be desperate about it though, since its early game content theres not much people who runs it, so forming the party might take a while.

Quest 1/5: Clear Forgotten Elrian Sanctum 1 time.

Quest 2/5: Clear Forgotten Elrian Sanctum 5 times.

Quest 3/5: Gather 20 Materials by Killing Skin Splitters.

Skin Splitters are found on Water Elrian Sanctum, Elrianode City, and El Tower Defense (Story Mode included), Elrianode City being the best place to do it IF you have a 2/3 Players Party. Else, if youre running solo, El Tower Defense is the best option.
The Materials you get from the quest, are transferable via Luriel Bank, but the materials are different for Noah/Ain, and Eve/Add.

Quest 4/5: Clear Elrianode City 3 times.

Quest 5/5: Clear El Tower Defense 5 times (Story Mode included).

After Completing Quest 5/5, you will advance to 3rd Class, which will unlock Force Skills. Force Skills are divided into 3 categories. Passives, Actives, and Mods.

- Passives, as the name indicates, are force skills that you can equip to give passive stats to your character.

- Actives, are an special type of skills that doesnt get affected by the Tenacity/Strength/Bravery/All Skill Damage Traits, and are not meant to be used for dpsing, but for certain situations.

- Mods, are modifications/alterations of a character skill, which can change the Skill Damage, Hitbox, Cast time, and Effects.

Force Skills have 3 Levels, Rare (lv1), Elite (lv2) & Unique (lv3), which can be upgraded with [IDK THE NAME] for lv1 → lv2 and [IDK EITHER] for lv2 → lv3.

After using a force skill item you won't be able to upgrade them unless you buy a Force Skill Extractor (via Camilla) *

Henir's Time and Space

You can find this dungeon on: Dungeon List → Special, Henir's Time and Space is alike to a "Boss Rush" with 2 difficulties, Normal Mode (20 Bosses) and Challenge Mode (6 Bosses, Higher HP than Normal Mode).

Your task here is to Clear Henir's Time and Space Stage 20 (Normal Mode), this will let you complete 4 quests from Dr. Pho's x Guide, Lesson x rewarding you the Force Skills:

Head Hunter (Passive)
Physical/Magical Power Decrease: 16% / 20% / 24%
Damage to Boss Monsters Increase: 40% / 60% / 80%

Eroding Energy (Passive)
Max MP Increase: 50 / 80 / 100
After 3 seconds of not attacking / being attacked recover 3% of your Max MP per Second
